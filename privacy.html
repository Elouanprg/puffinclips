PuffinClips — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-14

1. Local-only application

PuffinClips is a desktop application that runs entirely on your machine. All data (analyzed VODs, generated clips, configuration, OAuth tokens, statistics) is stored locally in a SQLite database and local files on your computer. The app does not send any of your personal data to remote servers operated by the author.

2. Data we process

The app processes the following data, strictly on your local machine:

  • Twitch VOD metadata and chat logs (fetched via the official Twitch API)
  • Video clips you generate
  • TikTok account information (display name, avatar, follower count) and the list of your published videos with their public statistics (views, likes, comments, shares), if you choose to connect your TikTok account
  • Configuration you provide (streamer handles, detection thresholds, API keys)

3. Third-party APIs

PuffinClips communicates directly with the Twitch API and the TikTok Open API from your machine, using credentials you have provided yourself. These calls are subject to the respective privacy policies of Twitch and TikTok.

4. OAuth tokens

When you connect your TikTok account, the access token and refresh token returned by TikTok are stored locally in the app's SQLite database. They are never transmitted to anyone else. You can revoke access at any time from your TikTok account settings or by clicking "Disconnect" inside PuffinClips.

5. No tracking, no analytics

PuffinClips does not collect telemetry, analytics, crash reports, or any usage data. The app makes no network call beyond those to Twitch and TikTok required for its features.

6. Children

PuffinClips is not directed at children under 13.

7. Contact

For any question regarding this policy, open an issue on the GitHub repository.
